Blockchain


The most amazing thing about Bitcoin's blockchain is that Satoshi Nakamoto, Bitcoin's creator, made it opensource! That's right. This revolutionary piece of technology is free!

Just go to Github's Bitcoin page and you can look at Bitcoin's code. Out of this transparency a community, an industry and all of the other cryptocurrencies were born. Bitcoin is truely a cryptocurrency for the people.
